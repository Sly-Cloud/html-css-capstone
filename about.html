PSYCH AND MENTAL HEALTH | SPOKEN WORDS CONFERENCE 2021

This is an bi-annual Psychology Conference that brings togther prominent Psychologists in the world, to share knowledge, tackle problems and seek solutions to impending dangers of mental health. This year's conference would be in a conducive environment of Sheraton Hotels, Abuja, Nigeria. It would hold for 3days, from 17th - 20th of October, 2021. For this year, we'll be expecting a host of professional in the field of Mental Health and there would discussion time, where we socialize and learn.
